Find your next role with Innovation RS

Send your CV once, and we’ll match your skills to relevant roles across our employer network. Clear, fast, and transparent.

How it works

Email your CV and a few details to cv@innovation-rs.com. We’ll add your profile to our database, map your strengths to open roles, and connect you directly with employers looking for your expertise.

What to include

  • Full name and phone / WhatsApp.
  • Location and relocation preferences.
  • Target roles / seniority (e.g., Frontend Engineer, Data Analyst).
  • Key skills / stack and years of experience.
  • Availability and salary expectations (optional).
  • Portfolio / GitHub links, if relevant.
